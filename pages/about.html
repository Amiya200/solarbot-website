🎯 Our Mission

Building the Future of Solar Maintenance

India is racing toward 500 GW of solar capacity by 2030. But the secret no one talks about: dirty panels silently kill up to 40% of that potential every year.

Manual cleaning is expensive, slow, and dangerous. Existing robotic solutions are either too costly or too limited. We saw an opportunity to do it right — and SolarBot Pro was born.

We're engineers who care deeply about renewable energy, robotics, and building things that actually work. Every component of SolarBot Pro has been tested in real-world conditions — on rooftops, on farms, in the dust-heavy summers of North India.

📅 Our Journey

FROM IDEA TO PRODUCT

💡
2022 — Q3
The Idea

Aditya visits a 500kW solar farm in Rajasthan and notices panels covered in dust. The operator is paying ₹40,000/month for manual cleaners. The idea for an autonomous cleaner is born.

🔬
2023 — Q1
First Prototype

The founding team builds the first working prototype with a basic roller and IR sensors. Tests on flat panels in the lab confirm 70%+ dust removal efficiency.

🌊
2023 — Q3
Water System Added

The three-stage cleaning system — spray, roller, wipe — is developed and tested. Dust removal jumps to 90%+. First field tests on a rooftop installation in Delhi.

🚀
2024 — Q2
SolarBot Pro Launch

SolarBot Pro officially launches with ESP32 Wi-Fi, MPU6050 gyroscope, and the full feature set. First 10 commercial installations. 95% dust removal rate confirmed in field conditions.

📈
2025 — Present
Growing Fleet

50+ units deployed across India. Fleet management software in development. Expanding to agrivoltaic and utility-scale solar farm partnerships.
