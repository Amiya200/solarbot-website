Will SolarBot work on my panel brand? +
Yes — SolarBot Pro is compatible with all major solar panel brands sold in India including Adani, Waaree, Vikram, Renewsys, Loom Solar, Canadian Solar, and more. The rail system attaches to the panel frame, not the panel itself. If you have a panel, it works.
Does it really need zero water? +
Completely waterless. The microfiber brushes are engineered to lift and carry dry dust and particulates off the panel surface without scratching the glass. No water, no chemicals, no residue. Ideal for Rajasthan, Gujarat, and other high-dust regions where water is scarce.
What if SolarBot falls off the panel? +
Every SolarBot comes with a dual safety tether system — two independent stainless steel cables secure it to the rail. Even in extreme wind conditions it cannot fall. And if anything ever goes wrong within 2 years, we replace it. No questions asked.
How does it charge itself? +
SolarBot has a built-in micro solar panel on its top surface that charges its battery during daylight hours. It typically cleans early morning and charges throughout the day. One full day of sunlight = enough charge for a full week of scheduled cleanings. Zero electricity cost.
How much efficiency will I actually gain? +
It depends on how dirty your panels are. In our customer data: panels cleaned within 1 month → 10–15% gain. Panels not cleaned in 3–6 months → 20–30% gain. Panels ignored for over a year → 35–40%+ gain. Most customers are shocked by how much they were losing. Use our ROI calculator above to see your specific numbers.
Do I need WiFi or internet for it to work? +
No internet required. The cleaning schedule is programmed once via Bluetooth and stored directly on the device. SolarBot runs autonomously — no internet, no WiFi, no cloud dependency. The app is optional for monitoring, not required for operation. Works perfectly in rural areas.
What's the installation process like? +
One-time install that takes under 10 minutes. The rail clips to your panel frame — no drilling, no permanent modifications. Every SolarBot comes with all tools needed plus a free 30-minute video call with our engineer who walks you through the install step by step.
