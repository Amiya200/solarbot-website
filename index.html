⚙ Working Principle

HOW SOLARBOT PRO WORKS

Six intelligent steps from panel edge to edge — fully automated, fully thorough.

01
🚀
Deploy & Position

SolarBot Pro is placed at one edge of the solar panel array. The ESP32 controller initializes all systems — motors, sensors, pump, and roller — completing a startup diagnostic in under 3 seconds.

02
💧
Activate Cleaning Systems

The water pump activates via relay, sending a fine mist through precision nozzles. Simultaneously, the microfiber roller begins rotating and the wiper blade positions itself for a three-stage clean.

03
🤖
Navigate & Clean

The rover moves in a systematic S-pattern path. The L298 motor driver provides precise speed control via PWM signals, ensuring even coverage across the panel surface at optimal cleaning speed.

04
👁
Edge & Obstacle Detection

Four IR sensors (front × 2, rear × 2) continuously scan for panel edges at 50ms intervals. When an edge is detected, the rover halts, turns, shifts laterally, and begins the next cleaning strip.

05
🧭
Stability Monitoring

The MPU6050 gyroscope tracks tilt, vibration, and orientation in real time. If abnormal incline or slippage is detected, SolarBot Pro auto-corrects or safely stops and sends an alert.

06
✅
Complete & Report

Once full coverage is achieved, SolarBot Pro parks itself, shuts down pump and roller, and sends a completion report to your mobile dashboard — showing coverage %, water used, and cleaning time.

🌀
Microfiber Roller System

Industrial-grade microfiber roller physically lifts and removes dust, bird droppings, pollen, and fine particles. Unlike air blowers, the roller makes direct contact with the panel surface, achieving up to 95% dust removal. Soft enough to never scratch anti-reflective coatings.

💧
Precision Water Sprinkler System

Three relay-controlled water nozzles deliver a fine, even mist across the panel. The pump activates only when needed, conserving up to 80% more water than manual cleaning while tackling stubborn mud, mineral deposits, and sticky residues.

🪟
Smart Wiper Blade

After water spray and roller action, a precision rubber wiper blade removes all remaining moisture and loosened particles. This three-stage clean (wet → scrub → wipe) ensures panels are left streak-free and dry — no water spots, no residue.

🧭
MPU6050 Gyro Stability

Real-time tilt, pitch, and roll monitoring ensures optimal contact pressure on inclined panels (up to 35° tilt). Detects slippage and abnormal vibration instantly, auto-correcting trajectory or safely pausing and alerting the operator.

📡
ESP32 Wi-Fi Control Hub

The ESP32 microcontroller provides Wi-Fi connectivity for real-time monitoring, manual override, and OTA firmware updates. Access your rover via a web dashboard or mobile interface from anywhere on your network.

👁
4× IR Edge Detection

Quad IR sensors provide 360° edge awareness. Front sensors prevent the rover from driving off panel edges, while rear sensors ensure safe reversing. Sampling at 50ms intervals means even fast-approaching edges are caught in time.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before investing in SolarBot Pro.

What types of solar panels does it work on? +
SolarBot Pro is compatible with all standard monocrystalline and polycrystalline solar panels. It works on flat and inclined installations up to 35° tilt. The microfiber roller is certified safe for all major anti-reflective coatings — no scratching, no damage.
How much water does it consume per cleaning cycle? +
SolarBot Pro uses a precision spray system that consumes approximately 0.5–2 litres of water per standard panel pass, depending on soiling level. This is up to 80% less water than traditional manual cleaning with buckets and mops.
How is the rover connected and controlled? +
The ESP32 controller connects to your Wi-Fi network and hosts a lightweight web dashboard accessible from any browser or mobile device on the same network. You can start/stop cleaning, monitor progress, set schedules, and receive completion reports. OTA firmware updates are also supported.
Is it safe for use on rooftop panels? +
Yes. The 4× IR edge detection system prevents the rover from driving off panel edges. The MPU6050 gyroscope continuously monitors tilt and will automatically pause if abnormal movement or excessive tilt is detected. The rover also has a low centre of gravity for stability on inclined surfaces.
What maintenance does SolarBot Pro require? +
The microfiber roller sleeve should be washed or replaced every 3–6 months depending on usage intensity. The water reservoir needs refilling before each cycle. The wiper blade may need annual replacement. Software is maintained via OTA updates at no extra cost.
Can it operate on multiple panel rows? +
SolarBot Pro is designed to clean one panel or connected section per deployment. For large solar farms, multiple units can be deployed simultaneously and monitored from a single dashboard. Fleet management software is on our roadmap for 2025.
What is the power supply requirement? +
SolarBot Pro requires a 12V DC power supply — a standard 12V adapter, battery pack, or the solar installation's 12V auxiliary output. A buck converter provides stable 5V to all logic components. A battery-powered wireless variant is in development.
How do I get a quote or place an order? +
Use the contact form below or email us directly at hello@easybuddies.in. For bulk orders of 5 units or more, we offer significant discounts and can discuss custom configurations, branding, and installation support. We typically respond within 24 hours on business days.
